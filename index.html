Data Visualisation 2

Crime rate

The visualisation shows the crime rate in different countries, measured as the number of homicides per 100,000 people.

A few notable observations from the visualisation are noted in the map in the years 2002, 2010 and 2011.


GDP

The GDP per capita of different countries is shown in the bump chart.

Data used in the chart is based on the current (2024) US$.


Drug use

Finally, drug use prevalence is shown in the stacked bar chart.

Population on the y-axis represents percentage of population of that specific continent.
